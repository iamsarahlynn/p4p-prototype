The screening

Three non-invasive tests. One 20-minute visit.

ANS
Autonomic nervous system

Evaluates how the body automatically regulates heart rate, blood pressure, and circulation through guided breathing exercises.

~8 min
ABI
Ankle-Brachial Index

Compares blood pressure in the arms and legs to screen for peripheral artery disease and circulation problems.

~4 min
Sudomotor
Sweat-gland nerve function

Assesses small-fiber nerve function — often one of the earliest windows on developing neuropathy.

~4 min
